# Design Context for Education Plus

## Design Context

### Users
Students, university students, and self-learners using the platform for intensive study sessions, note-taking, and academic collaboration. They need a focused, distraction-free environment that supports deep work.

### Brand Personality
Professional, calm, focused. The interface should inspire confidence and help users concentrate without overwhelming them with competing visual elements.

### Aesthetic Direction
A refined, professional academic tool aesthetic. Think of a premium online learning platform—clean, sophisticated, with restrained use of color. Similar to Notion, Linear, or Figma in terms of visual restraint and professionalism.

Anti-references: Overly colorful edtech (Discord-like), trendy gradient-heavy designs, overstimulating interfaces with multiple competing accent colors.

### Design Principles
1. **Color restraint**: Primary single accent color (indigo) with neutral dominance. Remove competing colors (teal, blue accents).
2. **Subtle hierarchy**: Use weight, size, and space instead of bold colors and heavy shadows.
3. **Calm focus**: Reduce visual noise so students can concentrate. Heavy shadows and bright gradients are distracting.
4. **Professional sophistication**: Signal quality through restraint, not loudness. Think premium product, not novelty app.
5. **Functional clarity**: Maintain clear affordances and interaction states without sacrificing calmness.

### Theme
Light mode primary, with dark mode support. Both should feel professional and restful.
